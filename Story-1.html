The story of In Atmosphere takes place in a large dyson sphere-like structure located at the center of Bootes void. Being approximately ____ in diameter, it encompasses an inverted blackhole, ____ in size. The blackhole in question acts as a star to the also inverted world contained within the structure, of which supports a polar biosphere, featuring concerningly familiar yet different life. The structure itself is unknown in origin, with researchers from the Epsilon Eridani and Centauri star systems first entering it on August 30th, 2542. On November 9th, 2545 Conflict broke out inside and around the structure when gunfire was exchanged amongst the joint researchers, it is unknown who opened fire. The result of the initial firefight was a UCSC ship making an in atmosphere jump into the structure, causing stationed ECC forces to call for reinforcements from the nearest battlegroup.
